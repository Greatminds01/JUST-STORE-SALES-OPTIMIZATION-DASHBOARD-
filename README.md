## 🏬 Business Context

Just-store is a growing retail business that offers a diverse range of products to both individual and corporate customers across multiple geographies. As the company scales, it faces critical challenges in understanding its sales performance, customer behavior, and operational efficiency.


## ❗ Problem Statement

The company is currently dealing with:

- ⚠️ Sales performance varying significantly across regions  
- 🔄 High customer churn in certain product categories  
- 💸 Suboptimal product profitability and underperforming segments  
- 📉 Limited insight into sales trends over time for forecasting  
- ⚙️ Inconsistent data preparation leading to analytical inefficiencies  


## 🎯 Project Goals

The aim of this project was to:

- Explore and analyze sales performance by region, category, and time
- Identify high-performing and underperforming products/segments
- Optimize decision-making using actionable insights
- Design a Tableau dashboard for real-time data exploration and presentation


## 📌 Dashboard Focus Areas

- 📈 Key Metrics Tracked:  
  - Sales  
  - Profit  
  - Quantity Sold

- 🌍 Regional Breakdown:  
  Understand which regions are driving revenue vs. underperforming.

- 🗂️ Category & Segment Insights:  
  Discover which product categories are most profitable or need attention.

- 🕒 Time Series Analysis:  
  Reveal trends and seasonal patterns in monthly sales.

- 🧠 Filters and Interactivity:  
  Analyze data dynamically by region, category, or time period.


## 📊 Visualizations Used

- Bar Chart: Sales by Region & Category  
- Line Chart: Monthly Sales Trends  
- Pie Chart: Profit Distribution  
- Heatmap: Performance across Region and Category  
- Interactive Filters: Region, Category, Date Range  


## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- Tableau Desktop
- Sample Superstore Dataset
- Excel/CSV for Data Input
- Tableau Public (optional for publishing)


## 🧩 Insights & Recommendations

- Reallocate inventory and marketing efforts to underperforming regions
- Focus on high-profit categories like Technology and Office Supplies
- Address customer churn with loyalty strategies in weak categories
- Launch Q4-focused campaigns based on time-series trends
- Standardize data prep with Tableau Prep or consistent ETL practices
